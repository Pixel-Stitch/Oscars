Burns Supper at Oscar's Inn

23rd January 2020

A very Happy New Year to all our customers. We hope you’ve all had a wonderful Christmas and New Year. However, now the excitement and excess is behind us we all need something to look forward to and to brighten the dark January evenings. Look no further than Oscar’s Inn:-

BURNS SUPPER - THURSDAY 23rd JANUARY 2018

We are celebrating the bard’s birthday in style with a traditional supper of Cullen skink soup, haggis and Cranachan for dessert (vegetarian options are available). We will be hosting a whisky tasting (4 whiskies) during the evening and a short, humorous and, in parts, bawdy toast to Burns’ life, loves and works will be expertly delivered by our knowledgeable friend, Ron Woolmer. We hope to have a selection of fine Scottish ales for the occasion.

Places for what promises to be a fun and interesting evening are limited so book early to avoid disappointment. Tickets are £25ph and are available direct from Oscar’s Inn.

Contact us on 01636 918130 or enquiries@oscarsinn.co.uk for more details and to book your place(s).
